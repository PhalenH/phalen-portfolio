Who am I?

My name is Phalen Hazel, and I'm a full stack web developer. I have a passion for turning ideas into results through precise code, creative designs, and fluid user interactions and experiences.

I've spent my post-collegiate life working as a Physical Therapist assistant at skilled nursing facilities. This professional experience has sharpened my time management and leadership skills, tought me how to excel in high-stress environments, and allowed me to work with a variety of team members to achieve a common goal.

Outside of work and my studies I enjoy staying active, going to see live music, and caring too much about Philadelphia sports. When I can I like to push ny limits through distance running, skydiving, and skiing. I am always open to new experiences and learning new skills, life is short so might as well do all you can!
